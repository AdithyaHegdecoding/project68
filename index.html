About

My school is Kumarans

It is the best school in the whole banglore

The Sri Kumaran Children’s Home CBSE wing was established under the guidance of our former director, Smt. Meenakshi Balakrishnan, in 1995 at Doddakalasandra and shifted to a larger campus at Mallasandra in 2007.The school has a demonstrated 27-year-old history of continued academic excellence. It is part of the Sri Kumaran Group of Institutions, which has a 64 year old legacy of providing quality education to students in south Bangalore. The school is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), which is overseen and controlled by the Ministry of Education, Government of India. The school has classes from I to XII. The mission of the Sri Kumaran Group of Institutions is to provide holistic development and world-class education to all of its students. We aim not only for academic excellence but also for each of our students to grow into responsible citizens. While we are constantly improving our teaching methods to meet the needs of changing times, our core values persist in all our endeavours. Sri Kumaran Children's Home has received many awards and accolades for its continued academic excellence. Our school became a part of the network of Ashoka Changemaker Schools as it was conferred with the Ashoka Changemaker School Award in 2017. Our school also received the HP Innovation in Education Award in 2010. The students of Sri Kumaran Children's Home have received recognition for their hard work and dedication. Our school stood third in the country in the consecutive years of 2014 and 2015 for the National Financial Literacy Assessment Test conducted jointly by CBSE and NCFE. We won the Shell Junior National Science Scholars Award 2012, for the written scholarship examination conducted by Shell India and the British Council.

A message from principal


" Education is not a mere accumulation of facts, it is a preparation for life itself. Education and knowledge are two important aspects which will take students to great heights. However, a blend of wisdom and ethics develops the personality of students, mould their character, develops their skills and helps them cope with problems and challenges of the complex world of today.The aim of Kumaran’s is not only to make its students successful in life, but also to make them conscious of their duties and responsibilities towards their fellow citizens. In our pursuit of providing a holistic education, we have myriad activities for children to enrich their life skills, kindle their spirit of curiosity and to build and strengthen their interest in sports. We sincerely hope these activities not only help them enjoy their school life but will aid in their “journey of life”. It is our sincere effort to uphold the name of Kumarans and strive relentlessly to help students grow in stature academically and more importantly as good contributors to society. We invest in an environment called Home. I sincerely thank all the teachers and colleagues for working in a supportive environment as a team, making the atmosphere in the school a pleasurable one. I thank parents for their relentless support in helping us get the best out of our students. This encouragement makes all our efforts worthwhile. Mrs. Chandrakala K Principal "
